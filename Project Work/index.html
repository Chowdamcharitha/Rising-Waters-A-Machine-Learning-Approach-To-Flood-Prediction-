About Flood Prediction Forecastation

Flood prediction with XGBoost involves collecting historical weather, river, and terrain data, preprocessing it for consistency, engineering features like rainfall averages and terrain characteristics, training an XGBoost model on the data, and evaluating its accuracy using metrics like MAE and RMSE. The model can then make real-time flood forecasts, aiding in disaster preparedness and risk mitigation. Continuous model refinement and updating with new data ensure its reliability over time, helping authorities and communities better respond to flood threats and minimize damage.
